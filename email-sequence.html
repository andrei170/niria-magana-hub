Post-sequence handling

Applicants who apply: Routed to a 30-minute information call with Yadi (or a VA-screened pre-call if volume scales). Cohort acceptance decided within 48 hours of the call.

Non-applicants who reply: Routed to a personal reply from Yadi or a designated team member. Common objection responses pre-written:

  • "Too expensive": Hold the line on $3,000. Offer the (future) self-paced course as the alternative. Don't discount the cohort.
  • "Wrong timing": Confirm next cohort batch date and offer to add them to the waitlist.
  • "I don't think it's for me": Thank them, ask if they know someone who'd be a fit, leave the door open.

Non-repliers who don't apply: Auto-rolled into the weekly content email cadence (see content-strategy.md). The warm-list nurture is the first touch; the weekly content email is the ongoing nurture.

Send mechanics

  • Platform: Whatever Yadi uses now. If migrating to GoHighLevel (per the June 3 call), build the sequence in GHL and trigger off a list tag.
  • Send time: 7:00 AM Pacific (her market). Tuesday for the first send, then matching cadence day-of-week for emails 2-5.
  • From-name: "Yadi from Wealthy Beginnings" (matches the brand-and-personal hybrid register)
  • Reply-to: wealthybeginings@gmail.com (or a dedicated yadi@ alias if she sets one up)
  • List hygiene: Pre-send, scrub the list for hard bounces and inactive emails. Sending to a clean list keeps deliverability high through the sequence.

Voice / register checks per email

Before deploying, run each email through the copy-strategy.md voice register checklist:

  • Zero em-dashes (only the sign-off should use the "- Yadi" pattern, no em-dashes anywhere else)
  • Zero banned phrases (no "transform", "unlock", "elevate", "level up", "passive income", "guaranteed", "six figures")
  • Verb-led action language throughout
  • Nikki as the proof, Yadi as the operator
  • Honest math with qualifier baked in
  • No promise of specific outcomes
  • Compliance disclaimer present where income figures appear
